KDIC Radio Streaming

About

The KDIC Streaming App is a convenient way for anyone to tune into their favorite radio station, KDIC, based out of Grinnell College, IA. The app allows you to pause and play the station, and should eventually pull metadata from the stream to display. On top of streaming KDIC, the app also brings the user content from the KDIC website, such as the program schedule.
